GravyGallery

GravyGallery is a decentralized community attempting to revoluntionize the art community. GravyGallery is an online art community that brings artists, buyers, and galleries together from across the world. By creating a decnetralized community, artists and buyers can avoid going through third parties such as auction houses to receive proper value for the work that is created. The platform is currently under development and set to be released in the Summer of 2018. The Python Fund bought GravyGallery and is currently working with the team preparing it for its release.
