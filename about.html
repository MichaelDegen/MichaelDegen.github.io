About us

The more you know about our services, the better we can serve your needs

The Python Fund is a private investment group focusing on building decentralized communities

Our team focuses on helping early stage companies that want to create decentralized communities using blockchain technology. We want to help build these communities with the goal of sustainable development of online communities.

In addition to helping consult these companies, we also research, analyze, and invest in these communities. We believe that decentralized communities can empower millions of people and we seek to help these companies do so. For business inquiries please see the contact page
